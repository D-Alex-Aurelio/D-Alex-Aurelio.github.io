CRM Sales Account Analysis

Go to Streamlit Application

Objectives

To create a web application for the management of accounts in the dataset given.

Key Findings

  • All accounts in the analysis are located in the United States.
  • The oldest company in this analysis is Konex.
  • Treequote is a subsidiary of Sonron.
  • Bubba Gump has the highest win rate in closed deals with 66.67% but also the lowest number of closed deals with 54 deals.
  • Hatfan has the lowest win rate in closed deals with 56.58%
  • Hatfan and Treequote has the higher number of deals in progress which is 9 deals.
  • Konex has 3 future/prospective deals, which is the highest number out of all the accounts.

Approach

  1. Obtained the data from Maven Analytics - Data Playground.
  2. Used the Python Library Streamlit to create the web application.
  3. A dropdown was used in the dashboard to pick which account to show.
  4. Basic information of the account was shown such as the name, sector, year established, etc.
  5. The deal information was divided into closed deals, ongoing deals, and future deals.
