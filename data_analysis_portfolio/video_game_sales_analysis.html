Video Game Sales Analysis

Objectives

This analysis focuses on the sales of the Wii, PlayStation 3 (PS3), and Xbox 360 (X360) of a particular store.

Key Findings

  • PS3 had the most variety of games sold the most with 42 game titles sold, while the Wii had the least variety of games sold with 1 game title sold.
  • Shooter games on the X360 generated the most total sales.
  • Adventure games on the PS3 received the highest average critic score.

Approach

  1. Obtained the data from Maven Analytics - Data Playground
  2. Removed unneeded columns from the data set.
  3. Filtered the data to focus on games for the PS3, Wii, and X360.
  4. A heatmap was used to analyze the total sales and critic scores per console-genre pair.

1. Game Titles Sold per Console

The count of game titles for each console are shown below. From the bar plot, PS3 had the most variety of games sold the most with 42 game titles sold while the Wii only had one game title sold.

2. Sum of Total Sales and Average Critic Scores

Total sales and critic scores are shown below. Shooter games are popular with having 37 million and 47 million total sales for PS3 and X360, respectively.

Despite not having the most sales, the adventure games of PS3 had the highest average critic score.

3. Sum of Total Sales vs Average Critic Scores

To show the relationship between critic score and total sales, a scatter plot was used. As shown in the plot below, higher critic scores is related to higher total sales per console.
