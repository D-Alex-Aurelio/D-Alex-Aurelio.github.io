Restaurant Order Analysis

Go to Plotly Dashboard

Objectives

This analysis will look into the daily trend, day of the week trend, and average number of orders of a given restaurant.

Key Findings

  • The maximum orders per day is 85 orders and it occured on Feb 1, 2023.
  • The minimum orders per day occured on Feb 22, 2023 with 35 orders.
  • With outliers, Wednesday has the most variablity when it comes to restaurant orders.
  • Without outliers, Sunday has the most variablity when it comes to restaurant orders.
  • The peak hours of the restaurant are during 12:00 (12:00 PM) and 17:00 (5:00 PM).

Approach

  1. Obtained the data from Maven Analytics - Data Playground.
  2. The Python Library Plotly was used to create a dashboard.
  3. A line plot was used to show the number of orders per day. Additionally, a mean line was plotted to show the average daily orders.
  4. For added information in the daily orders, the minimum, maximum, and median number of daily orders were shown.
  5. The analysis of the orders per day of the week utilized a box and whisker plot to show the corresponding distribution and statistics of orders.
  6. A bar plot was used to plot the number of average orders per hour. From the plot, the peak hours were identified.
