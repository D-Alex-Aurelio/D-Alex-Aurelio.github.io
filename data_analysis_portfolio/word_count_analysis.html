Word Count Analysis

Objectives

This analysis seeks to determine the occurrence of “civil” or “engineering” on the Simple Wikipedia page about Civil Engineering.

Key Findings

  • The word “civil” occurred more than the word “engineering” on the webpage about Civil Engineering.
  • The words “engineering” and “engineers” occurred more than the word “civil” on the webpage.

Approach

  1. The requests library of Python was used to obtain information from the Simple Wikipedia page.
  2. In order to make the obtained information usable, the BeautifulSoup library of Python was used.
  3. To count the desired words for the analysis, a Counter object from the collections library was used.
  4. Plotting the count of the words utilized the matplotlib.pyplot library.

1. Civil vs Engineering

From the bar plot shown below, the word "civil" occured 15 times, which is more than the occurrence of the word "engineering" which occured 7 times on the Simple Wikipedia page.

2. Civil vs Engineering and Engineers

Upon adding a related word to "engineering", which is "engineers", the word count increased to 17 which is more than the occurrence of the word "civil" on the Simple Wikipedia page.
