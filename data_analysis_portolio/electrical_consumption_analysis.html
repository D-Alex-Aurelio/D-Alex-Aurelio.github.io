Electrical Consumption Analysis

Since I am coming from a background in civil engineering, designing utilities such as roads, and water supply is something I am familiar with. However, I have yet to explore a dataset involving electrical consumption. I wonder what insights I can gather from this data set from Maven Analytics.

1. Inspection of Data Set

Table 1.1 Number of Rows and Columns

Rows 52,416
Columns 9

Table 1.2 Field Names with Description and Data Type

Field Name Description Data Type
Datetime Date and time of recording Datetime
Temperature Temperature in Celsius Double
Humidity Concentration of water vapor in the air Double
WindSpeed Speed of the wind in kilometers per hour Double
GeneralDiffuseFlows Measurement of how much emissions gases diffuse into the broader atmosphere Double
DiffuseFlows Measurement of how much emissions gases diffuse into the local atmosphere Double
PowerConsumption_Zone1 Power consumption in Kilowatt-Hours in Power Zone 1 Double
PowerConsumption_Zone2 Power consumption in Kilowatt-Hours in Power Zone 2 Double
PowerConsumption_Zone3 Power consumption in Kilowatt-Hours in Power Zone 3 Double

From Table 1,2, we can see there are three zones included in this data set which are labeled Zone 1, Zone 2, and Zone 3. Additionally, there are other factors included in this time series data set such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, general diffuse flows, and diffuse flows. Due to my lack of knowledge regarding the effects of diffuse flows, I will omit it from my analysis.
